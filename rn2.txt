About the job
Job Title: Mid-Level React Native Developer

Department: AI

Reports to: Development Manager



Role Overview



We are looking for a Mid-Level React Native Developer to join our development team and contribute to building scalable, high-performance mobile applications for telecom and digital services.

You will work on consumer-facing and enterprise mobile apps used across multiple markets, collaborating closely with backend, DevOps, product, and UX teams.

This role is ideal for a developer who is hands-on, delivery-focused, and comfortable working in fast-moving telecom environments.



Key Responsibilities



Mobile Application Development

Design, develop, and maintain cross-platform mobile applications using React Native.
Implement clean, maintainable, and reusable UI components.
Translate UI/UX designs and business requirements into high-quality mobile features.
Integration & APIs

Integrate mobile apps with RESTful APIs, authentication services, and backend systems.
Work with telecom-related APIs (e.g., billing, authentication, user identity, notifications).
Handle secure data exchange, token-based authentication, and session management.
Performance & Quality

Optimize application performance, responsiveness, and stability.
Identify and fix bugs, crashes, and memory leaks.
Write unit tests and participate in code reviews to maintain high code quality.
Collaboration

Collaborate with backend developers (Java, Node.js, Python), product managers, and designers.
Participate in sprint planning, estimations, and technical discussions.
Support production deployments and post-release improvements.


Education

Bachelor’s degree in computer science, Software Engineering, Information Technology, or a related field.
 Relevant certifications in mobile development or cloud technologies are a plus.


Required Skills & Qualifications



Technical Skills

3–5 years of experience in mobile application development.
Strong experience with React Native and modern JavaScript (ES6+).
Solid understanding of React hooks, state management, and component lifecycle.
Experience with Redux, Zustand, or similar state management libraries.
Hands-on experience consuming REST APIs.
Familiarity with mobile app deployment on Google Play Store and Apple App Store.
Knowledge of Git and collaborative development workflows.
Mobile & Platform Knowledge

Understanding of Android and iOS platform differences.
Experience with mobile permissions, background tasks, push notifications, and deep linking.
Familiarity with app security best practices (secure storage, token handling).


Nice-to-Have Skills

Experience in telecom, fintech, or digital services domains.
Knowledge of TypeScript.
Experience with CI/CD pipelines for mobile apps.
Familiarity with analytics, crash reporting tools, and feature flags.
Exposure to billing, subscription flows, or authentication systems.


What We Offer

Opportunity to work on large-scale telecom and digital platforms across multiple countries.
Exposure to real-world, high-traffic production systems.
Collaborative, technically strong engineering environment.
Career growth within a fast-growing international telecom group.
