About the job
Priority Soft is a development agency specializing in web and mobile applications. We are looking for an independent development partner to join our team long-term. We have an immediate project starting that will last at least 3 months, with ongoing collaboration on multiple future projects as a valued member of our development team.


Key Responsibilities:

Development of full-stack web applications using React and Next.js based on Figma designs
Backend development using NestJS or other TypeScript-based frameworks
Collaborating on various projects as part of our extended development team


The Ideal Candidate Has:

Minimum of five years of hands-on experience building React and full-stack TypeScript applications
Strong proficiency in Next.js for frontend development
Experience with NestJS or other TypeScript backend frameworks (Express, Fastify, etc.)
Excellent communication skills
Availability of at least 25 hours per week


What We Offer:

As our development partner, you will have the opportunity to:
Work with complete autonomy
Develop innovative and exciting applications across multiple projects
Be part of a team that values your talent and creativity
Build a long-term partnership with consistent project flow


We value exceptional developers and provide them with an environment where they can grow. We believe in absolute autonomy and the opportunity to work on interesting projects.


Compensation:

$20/hour


If you are ready to become a trusted development partner and work in an environment that encourages growth and long-term collaboration, we'd love to hear from you.
